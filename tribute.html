Dr. Mario

The man who could maybe save a billion lives if he were real.

Facts about Dr. Mario:

  • His first known appearance was on July 27, 1990. At that time he was already an adult and a doctor.
  • It is unknown what his qualifications are or where he obtained his degrees.
  • As no one can actually swallow his medicine, he mostly throws it at enemies to inflict harm, with surprising success.
  • To collect payment from his patients, he punches them into the air, somehow causing large golden coins to appear.
  • Although short in stature, his jumping ability surpasses all known records.
  • He looks familiar, but no one can quite put their finger on it.
